Event description

Description

Choose from Two Trainings:

Option 1 - Standard Training Session at 8:00 am:

Are you an administrator, instructional coach, or teacher who wants to find new ways to use USATestprep in the classroom? Are you interested in viewing student progress or tracking data for your school? Are you a new user looking for an overview of the site’s resources and features? If so, this workshop is a great way to learn more about USATestprep and all that we have to offer your school.

Who: All administrators, teachers, and staff who wish to maximize the effectiveness of USATestprep at their school.

Join us for breakfast and learn how to:

    • Incorporate a variety of resources into your lesson plans

    • Create custom assessments to measure student mastery by standard

    • Maximize USATestprep's Dot Rank system for data-driven progress monitoring

    • Utilize individual learning pathways to improve student achievement

    This training is helpful for teachers and administrators who want to:

    • Discover newly-released features

    • Become familiar with USATestprep’s capabilities

    • Get the most out of USATestprep at the classroom, building, and district levels

    We will also cover administrator resources, Q&A, and future plans. A laptop or tablet is required.

A catered continental breakfast break (pastries, juice, fruit, and coffee) is included.

Option 2 - Advanced Training Session at 12:30 pm:

Are you looking for innovative ways to utilize USATestprep in your classroom? Would you like to learn creative strategies to strengthen student mastery of standards? USATestprep is offering an advanced user meeting to showcase resources and features that will maximize the effectiveness of the site for your school.

Who: All administrators, teachers, and staff who wish to explore creative and advanced ways to maximize student achievement and instructional time with USATestprep.

What: In-depth, hands-on training on using USATestprep, including:

  • Extensive Coverage of Custom Assessment Features
  • Review of Built-In Site Accommodations and RTI Strategies
  • Focused Resource Instruction with Differentiation Techniques
  • In-depth Lessons on Self-Directed Student Learning Models
  • Q&A, Reflection, and Future Plans
  • A Laptop or Tablet Device is required.

Includes a boxed lunch.

Both Sessions Include:

  • Beverages Throughout
  • Super Awesome USATestprep Swag
